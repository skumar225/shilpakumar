WHO AM I?

Hello. My name is Shilpa. I'm a full-stack web developer based in the great city of San Francisco, CA.

Originally from Dallas, Texas, I've also lived and studied in Hong Kong, Cape Town, South Africa, Seoul, South Korea, and Mumbai, India. I'm a Smith College graduate, where I studied economics and psychology.

I'm a recent graduate of General Assembly's 12 week Web Development Immersive course, where I got the opportunity to dive into web development, coding in both server and client side languages. My projects specifically made use of Javascript/jQuery, Rails, and Node.js.

A music enthusiast, I play guitar, piano, and the ukulele in my spare time. I also like dogs. And coffee. And cheesecake. Not necessarily in that order.
